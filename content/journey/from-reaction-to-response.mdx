## Principle

Reacting reinforces old patterns.  
Responding creates new ones.

---

## Teaching

Notice the frustrations that repeat in your life.

The same arguments.  
The same disappointments.  
The same emotional triggers.

Kabbalah teaches that reactive behavior feeds chaos.  
Each reaction strengthens the pattern that created it.

But response is different.

Response is conscious.  
It is chosen.  
It interrupts repetition.

When you respond instead of react, you begin rewriting your internal code.

That is real transformation.

---

## Practice

Identify one recurring frustration today.

When it appears, choose a response that is just 1% different than usual.

That small shift begins a new pattern.

---

## Reflection

- What reactions define my current patterns?
- What would my higher self choose?
- What would a slightly better response look like?
